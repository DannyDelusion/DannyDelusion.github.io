About

Welcome! We are the Video Game Vanguards!!! 

We are a discord of streamers and gamers with the intent of bringing people together! We are dedicated to support content creators big and small who mainly stream on Twitch, but we also welcome those who use other streaming services!

Video Game Vanguards is also for those who may not stream, but seek an active community playing the latest games, fan favorites, most talked about multiplayer games, or just want to have fun.

Our main goal is to make this a welcoming environment for anyone who has ever picked up a controller, mashed WASD and left click to death, or aspires to become affiliate and ultimately partner. We hope you stay and be part of a community that's bigger than any one of us!

Discord Features:

  • Discord Leveling System (rewarding users who chat and network with each other)
  • Community Game Nights (Playing games on stream with each other)
  • Lurker.tv integration (get more views for your stream, and meet others with similar interests)
  • Giveaways & Prizes (Games/Giftcards/Tech)
  • Various Skillsets (Help and advice from all levels of knowledge)
  • GFX/Tech Artists (Advice & Marketplace)
  • Vast International Community
  • And much much more

Rules

VGV is a community that lives to help each other grow online. while in Discord we strive to be an adult community who can have a good laugh with each other at the same time

This is the rules we have in place to keep things chill, everyone wants to get ahead and get along, and importantly, talking to each other civilly! The more we grow the more you grow!

  • Minimum Age: 16
  • Be kind and supportive of your fellow Vanguards, we're all here to grow
  • Respect the mods. Do not fight the mods in #general-chat. If there is an issue, please talk to them in DMs. There are rules for a reason and they have been tasked to enforce them.
  • Use the proper channels for your chat, especially for links and promotion
  • No racism of any kind
  • No explicit nicknames
  • No referral/Discord or kickback links (if you are supporting a charity like AbleGamers, or something like that, get in contact with a staff member so we can work it out!)
  • No leeching.
  • No trolling or toxicity
